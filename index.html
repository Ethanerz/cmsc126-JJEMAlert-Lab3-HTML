Most Popular Anime of the 2000s – 2020s

A celebration of the greatest anime series across three iconic decades.

From the action-packed arcs of the early 2000s to the emotional masterpieces of the 2020s, anime has captured the hearts of millions worldwide. This site covers the most beloved and highly rated series that defined each generation of anime fans. Whether you're a longtime otaku or just getting started, there's something here for everyone.


Highlights by Decade

2000s

The golden age of long-running shonen. Epic battles, unforgettable characters, and stories that shaped a generation.

Notable Titles:
▸ Naruto (2002)
▸ Fullmetal Alchemist (2003)
▸ One Piece (1999–)
▸ Death Note (2006)
▸ Bleach (2004)
2010s

A decade of diversity and innovation. From dark fantasy to slice-of-life, anime expanded into bold new territory.

Notable Titles:
▸ Attack on Titan (2013)
▸ Sword Art Online (2012)
▸ My Hero Academia (2016)
▸ Re:Zero (2016)
▸ Demon Slayer (2019)
2020s

A new era of cinematic quality and emotional depth. Modern anime pushes the boundaries of storytelling and animation.

Notable Titles:
▸ Jujutsu Kaisen (2020)
▸ Frieren: Beyond Journey's End (2023)
▸ Chainsaw Man (2022)
▸ Spy x Family (2022)
▸ Oshi no Ko (2023)
